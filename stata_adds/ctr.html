The CTR Stata module
 
The CTR Stata module can be used to estimate the CTR-Gini index, which is more appropriate in presence of some negative incomes and positive average income.
 
Reference:
Chen, C.-N.; Tsaur, T.-W.; Rhai, T.-S. (1982): The Gini coeficient and negative income. Oxford Economic Papers, 34, 473-478.
