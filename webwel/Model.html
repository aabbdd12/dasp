WELCOM Features

  • Estimates distributional impacts of changes in market structure;
  • Estimates standard errors and confidence intervals based on survey design;
  • Options for different market structures:
    • Monopoly;
    • Oligopoly: Cournot-Nash equilibrium;
    • Oligopoly: Partial Oligopoly Model.
  • Produces automated tables and figures with main results;
  • Customizes outputs by population groups or particular variables;
