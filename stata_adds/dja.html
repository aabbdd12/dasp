The DJA Stata module
 
The DJA Stata modules enables to decompose the difference in inequality between two distrbutions into vertical equity (VE), horizontal inequity (HI) and reranking (RE) components, the used approach is that  of Duclos, Jalbert amd Araar (2003).
 
Reference:
Duclos, Jean-Yves & Jalbert, Vincent & Araar, Abdelkrim, 2003. "Classical Horizontal Inequity and Reranking: an Integrating Approach," Cahiers de recherche 0306, CIRPEE.
