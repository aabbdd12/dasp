The OGTPR Stata module
 
The OGTPR Stata module is designed to estimate the optimal group transfers for the optimal reduction of poverty using a fixed budget of transfers. The method is valid for all poverty indices of the FGT class. This module is mainly based on the developments of Araar and Tiberti (2017).
 

Reference:
Araar Abdelkrim and Tiberti Luca (2017), Optimal Targeting and Poverty Reduction, PEP-PMMA
technical note series 2017-06.
