Poverty
IFGT Estimates the FGT and the EDE-FGT indices and their standard errors taking full account of survey design.
   
DIFGT Estimates the difference between FGT indices and its standard error taking full account of survey design.
   
IMDPOV

 

 

 

 

 

 

 Estimates the multidimensional poverty indices and their standard errors taking full account of survey design. The estimated MD indices are:

[1] Chakravarty et al (1998) index;
[2] Extended Watts index;
[3] Multiplicative extended FGT index;
[4] Tsui (2002) index;
[5] Intersection headcount index.
[6] Union headcount index.
[7] Bourguignon and Chakravarty (2003) bidimensional index.
