DASP: New Modules and Options in version 2.3
   
IMDP_CMR

 

 Estimates the Chakravarty et al (1998) multidimensional poverty index and its standard error taking full account of survey design.

 
IMDP_EWI

 

 Estimates the Extended Watts  multidimensional poverty index and its standard error taking full account of survey design.

 
IMDP_MFI

 

 Estimates the Chakravarty et al (1998) multidimensional poverty index and its standard error taking full account of survey design.

 
IMDP_TSU

 

 Estimates the Tsui (2002)  multidimensional poverty index and its standard error taking full account of survey design.

 
IMDP_IHI

 

 Estimates the intersection headcount  multidimensional poverty index and its standard error taking full account of survey design.

 
IMDP_UHI

 

 Estimates the union headcount  multidimensional poverty index and its standard error taking full account of survey design.

 
IMDP_BCI

 

 

 Estimates the Bourguignon and Chakravarty (2003)  multidimensional poverty index and its standard error taking full account of survey design.

 
IMDP_AFI

 

 

 Estimates the Alkire and Foster (2007)  multidimensional poverty indices and their standard error taking full account of survey design.

 
IMODA

 

 

 

 Multiple Overlapping Deprivation Analysis indices:

The imoda DASP module produces a series of multidimensional poverty indices in order to show the incidence of deprivation in each dimension. Further, this application estimates the incidence of multi-deprivation in the different combinations of dimensions.
 

DASP Decomposition Modules
   
DFGTG2D

 

 Sectoral decomposition of change in FGT indices.

 
DMDAFG

 

 

 Decomposes Alkire and Foster (2007) multidimensional poverty indices by groups and provides standard errors on various elements of the decompositions.

 
DMDAFS

 

 

 Decomposes Alkire and Foster (2007) multidimensional poverty indices by dimensions using the Shapley value approach.
