The AJLDEC Stata module
 
The AJLDEC Stata modules enables to decompose the difference in inequality between two distrbutions into vertical equity (VE), horizontal inequity (HI) and reranking (RE) components, the used approach is that of Aronson Johnson and Lambert  (1994).
 
Reference:
Aronson, J. R., Johnson, P. and Lambert, P. J. (1994). Redistributive effect and unequal income tax treatment.  The Economic Journal, Vol. 104, pp. 262-270.
