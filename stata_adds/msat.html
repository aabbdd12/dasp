The MSAT post-estimation Stata module
 
The post estimation msat Stata module (and the mspredict_ar module) can be used after the use of the Stata command movestay to estimate the average treatment effect of the treated group, the untreated group and the whole population. The following reference discusses the endogeneous switching regression model and compares it with the popular PSM model.
 
Reference:
Araar, Abdelkrim (2015), The treatment effect: Comparing the ETR, ESR and PSM methods with artificial examples. PEP-PMMA technical note.
