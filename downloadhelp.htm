Downloading  DASP Package
The Stata package DASP is freely distributed. The user should be registered before downloading DASP.
  • To register, choose the item "Registration" and submit your request after completing the required information. You will receive your username and password instantly by email.
  • To download DASP, connect to the intranet service by choosing the item "Login" and  then select the item "Download DASP:  Distributive Analysis Stata Package".
